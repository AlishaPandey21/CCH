FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CollabHub.

How does CollabHub work?

CollabHub connects brands with influencers for marketing collaborations. Brands can post campaigns specifying their requirements, budget, and target audience. Influencers browse and apply to campaigns that match their niche. Once matched, both parties collaborate through our platform with secure contracts, messaging, and payments.

How do influencers get paid?

We use a secure escrow payment system. When a brand accepts an influencer, they deposit the agreed amount into escrow. Once the influencer delivers the content and it's approved by the brand, the payment is released. This protects both parties and ensures fair transactions.

Is it free to join as an influencer?

Yes! Influencers can join and browse campaigns completely free. We only charge a small platform fee (10%) on successful collaborations. This means you only pay when you get paid. There are no upfront costs or subscription fees for creators.

How do I find the right influencers for my brand?

Our smart search and AI matching system helps you find perfect matches. Filter by niche, follower count, engagement rate, location, platform, and more. You can also post a campaign and let influencers come to you. We verify all profiles to ensure quality and authenticity.

What if there's a dispute?

Our dedicated support team handles all disputes fairly. With escrow protection and clear contracts, issues are rare. If a dispute arises, we review the evidence from both parties and make a fair decision. We prioritize resolution and maintaining trust on our platform.

What platforms do you support?

CollabHub supports all major social media platforms including Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Twitch, and blogs. Influencers can connect multiple platforms to their profile to showcase their complete reach.
